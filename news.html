11/25 Community Vigil for Yang Song

TIME: November 25th at 2:30pm
PLACE: 135-32 40th Road in Flushing, Queens

Community members gather to remember and pay our respects to Yang Song, beloved daughter, sister, immigrant worker, sex worker.

Demand an end to the police terror of massage parlor workers and all sex working people! Respect, Dignity, and Self-determination for ALL immigrant workers NOW!

Red Canary:

Migrant Massage Workers Coalition

Inspired by our sister org, Butterfly, which started in Toronto in 2014, we are building a grassroots collective of Asian and migrant sex workers, and our allies. Offering legal and health services, Know-Your-Rights trainings, arts and activism workshops: we organize for safety, dignity, and justice. We build power to change the laws that keep our industry underground and put our lives at risk.
